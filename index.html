Sports for All: Breaking Barriers in India

Sport Summit
India 2023

The Sport Summit India 2023 is an exciting opportunity for sports enthusiasts, professionals, and organizations to come together and discuss how we can break barriers and create more inclusive and accessible sports environments in India.

29.08.2023(TUE)

Indira Gandhi Stadium Complex, New Delhi

Main Program

  • Opening Cermony

    The opening ceremony celebrates the power of sports to bring people together, and it sets the stage for an exciting and unforgettable event.

  • Speakers

    The speakers at the event are renowned sports personalities who have made significant contributions to their respective sports.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinion with experts.

  • Skill-Show

    The Skill-Show event is a testament to the incredible talent and athleticism of the world's top sportspeople.

  • SS Ignite

    Get opportunities to network with SS affiliates around the world, even after the summit.
