India 2023

India has always had a rich history and culture in sports, from ancient games like Kabaddi and Kho Kho to modern-day cricket and football. However, the potential of sports to transform the country goes beyond just entertainment and recreation. The Sports Summit India aims to explore and harness the power of sports to empower the nation in various ways.
